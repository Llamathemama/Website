Leadership Philosophy

The leadership style that resonates with me most is a supportive style, mixed with a democratic approach. I believe those two work well together because it allows for diversity of thought and organic care for the situation. People tend to care about something more if they feel their individual contribution is valued.

“The masses are the real heroes, while we ourselves are often childish and ignorant. Without this understanding, it is impossible to acquire even the most rudimentary knowledge.”
– Mao Zedong

This quote reminds me that leadership is fundamentally tied to valuing people and their voices. A true leader encourages alternative viewpoints and empowers the group.
